In order to find the right career path, you need to do some research about what you want to do. After reading those articles, try reaching out to someone in that field or joining a group that interests you. Remember, you don’t have to be perfect—just be yourself and stay open to learning from others. Every conversation is a chance to grow your network and learn something new.

Career Tips

  • For more information, please visit Career Link to find more job opportunties that would help you find the right career path.
  • Email us or come to our office to find a career coach that would help you find the right career path.
  • Join more connection events to talk and meet more people that would help you find the right career path.
